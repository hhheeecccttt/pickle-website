From Garden to Jar

Every step of our process is designed to deliver the freshest, most organic, and crunchiest pickles possible.

1

Plant & Grow

We start with organic seeds planted in nutrient-rich soil. Our crops are hand-tended and watered, without pesticides.

2

Hand Harvest

Vegetables are picked by hand at peak ripeness. From vine to brine in under 4 hours, that's the secret to our signature crunch.

3

Cold Brine

Our vegetables soak in a cold saltwater brine to draw out excess water. This keeps them firm and crisp while absorbing our custom signature spice blends.

4

Jar & Age

Each jar is packed by hand with vegetables, fresh herbs, garlic, and spices. Then we fill them with our signature vinegar brine and let them age to perfection.

5

Label & Deliver

Every jar gets a hand-written batch number and date. We deliver within 48 hours to ensure you get the freshest pickles possible.
